Preface

This book is the basic reading material for the course “Introduction to Data Analysis”, held at the Universtity of Osnabrück in the winter term of 2019, as part of the BSc Cognitive Science program. It introduces key concepts of data analysis from a frequentist and a Bayesian tradition. It uses R to handle, plot and analyze data. It relies on simulation to illustrate selected statistical concepts.
